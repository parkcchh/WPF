BRAND INFO

ALWAYS BESIDE YOU, EDIYA coffee

brand bean

The sincerity of a cup of coffee

Ediya Coffee, a pure domestic brand that does not pay overseas royalties, believes in providing consumers with good quality and delicious coffee at a reasonable price. Starting with the establishment of the Coffee Research Institute in 2010 to make more delicious coffee, we opened [Ediya Coffee Lab] in April 2016 with the theme of 'Coffee Research Center Communicating with Customers' and are conducting continuous research and development for better coffee.

CI of Ediya

EDIYA is the tribal name of Ethiopia, the birthplace of coffee, and at the same time means the continent's only emperor, and symbolizes EDIYA's vision to pursue the best coffee brand.

Ediya Roasting

  • Green Coffee selection

    We select high-quality products from each coffee producing region in the world and store them at low temperatures. Remove foreign substances (twigs, leaves, stones, etc.) that can affect the original taste and aroma of coffee.

  • Sample roasting

    Korea's best Q-grader (coffee taster) and coffee roaster (roaster) perform sample roasting to understand the characteristics of coffee.

  • Blending Cupping

    In order to provide more delicious coffee to customers, we conduct tests through several cuppings and blending.

  • Roasting the product

    The product is produced with a dedicated roasting machine so that you can enjoy the original taste and aroma of coffee.

  • Store supply

    The product is packaged quickly and supplied to Ediya stores during the period when the coffee is the freshest and richest in taste and flavor.

  • Customer sales

    Ediya Coffee, a coffee that impresses customers with the best baristas and service.

Win-win cooperation for happiness together

Ediya Coffee values win-win cooperation with customers, franchisees, and partners above all else. Ediya Coffee's 'win-win cooperation' has been built on a long-term basis based on honesty and trust. We provide our customers with our sincerity, guide franchisees to future happiness with various support policies, and are supplied with high-quality raw and subsidiary materials with strong trust built over a long period of time with partners.
