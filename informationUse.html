ediya Information Use

  • Membership registration information

  • - Our Ediya Store basically operates on a membership system.
  • - Membership registration is free.
  • - You can make purchases and use services when you sign up as a member.

  • Ordering Information

  • - Product search
  • - Add to shopping bag
  • - Member ID login
  • - Fill out the order form
  • - Select payment method and make payment
  • - Order success screen (order number)

  • Payment information

  • - In the case of large payments, the card company may call you to confirm for security reasons. During the verification process, if it is determined that the order is not a normal order, such as the use of a stolen card or an order under someone else's name, the order may be put on hold or canceled at our discretion.

  • Delivery information

  • - Delivery method: courier delivery
  • - Delivery area: Nationwide (Jeju/Island/Mountains costs borne by customer)
  • - Shipping cost: Free shipping
  • - Delivery period: within approximately 2-3 days

  • Order delivery

  • - Shipping is free, but some products have additional shipping costs.
  • - Additional shipping charges apply to Jeju Island and mountainous islands.
  • - Delivery takes approximately 2-3 days from the date of shipment, and may be delayed depending on the circumstances of the delivery company.
  • - Delivery service is suspended on weekends and public holidays.
  • - Delivery is excluded to some areas near military bases.
  • - Please request a change in delivery address through customer service.
  • - Cancellation or changes are not possible after entering the invoice.

  • Exchange/return information

  • - When exchanging/returning a product, you must contact the customer service center (070-7404-0295) and then send it in.
  • - Cancellation/change is not possible after the product has been shipped, so please choose carefully.
  • - In case of exchange/return due to simple change of mind, you will be responsible for the round-trip shipping fee for exchange (KRW 6,000) and return (KRW 3,500).
  • - Return address: Room temperature - Pasto, 691 Wonsul-ro, Baekam-myeon, Cheoin- gu, Yongin-si, Gyeonggi-do (3rd floor) Pasto Low temperature - Pasto, 691 Wonsul-ro, Baekam-myeon, Cheoin-gu, Yongin-si, Gyeonggi-do (1st basement floor) Pasto
  • - For more information, see the delivery and exchange return information at the bottom of the product detail page. Please note.

  • Refund information

  • - When requesting a refund, we will refund the amount paid within 3 business days after confirming the return.
  • - Refunds will be issued within 3 to 5 business days depending on the payment method (card company).

  • Other information

  • - In case of order cancellation, return or refund, the points used in the order will be refunded.
